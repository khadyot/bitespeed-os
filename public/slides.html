9 — Scalability

What Happens When Volume Doubles

The OS is designed so that additional accounts add zero marginal administrative overhead.

❌ Spreadsheet Model (Today)
2× rows to scan every morning
2× WhatsApp messages typed manually
2× mental filtering to find priority
2× weekly review prep time
Overhead scales linearly.
✅ OS Model (Proposed)
Priority Engine re-sorts automatically
WhatsApp Nudge drafts in one click
Escalation Engine catches every breach
Weekly Review aggregates with zero prep
Only conversations scale. Everything else is flat.
OM Capacity Guardrail
Hard cap of 25 active accounts per OM, enforced at intake. Risk-weighted: an OM with 15 accounts (3 Red) is flagged as more stressed than an OM with 20 accounts (0 Red).
